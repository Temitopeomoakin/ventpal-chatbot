Facing your fears
What do you think would happen?
What would happen to your anxiety if you faced a feared situation and stayed in it without using any unhelpful behaviours?
Most people would say that they wouldnâ€™t be able to cope.
The anxiety would just keep rising until eventually something terrible happens such as passing out or having a heart attack. We know this is in fact not the case.
There is a release of adrenaline when we become anxious which can impact our physical responses. But we also know that we can only produce so much of this. The physical symptoms will eventually reduce, once the adrenaline does.
