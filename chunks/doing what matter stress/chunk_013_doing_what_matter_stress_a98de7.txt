Part 1: GROUNDING
For example, if you are a parent or if you have responsibilities caring for others, what kind of person do you want to be?
• Loving
• Wise
• Attentive
• Committed
• Persistent
• Responsible
• Calm
• Caring
• Protective
• Courageous
So values describe the sort of person you want to be; how you want to treat yourself and others and the world around you.
