Part 2: UNHOOKING
The first two steps in unhooking are:
1. NOTICE
2. NAME
First you NOTICE that a thought or feeling has hooked you.
Then you NAME it. To name it, you silently say to yourself something like…
Here is Here is tightness in pressure in my chest. my forehead.
Here is a painful memory.
Here are fears about the future.
