How to recognise a panic attack
A ‘hot cross bun’ for a panic attack could look something like the below:
Thoughts
I’m having a heart attack
I’m losing control
I’m going to die
Physical
Feelings
Difficulty breathing,
Anxious, scared,
dizzy, chest pains,
ashamed, frustrated,
sweating, tingling,
upset nausea
Behaviours
Needing to escape,
avoiding/leaving situations
