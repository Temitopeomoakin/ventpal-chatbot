PsychologicalDistressWorkbook
This workbook is advanced and requires more focus and concentration than others. It will also take longer than other workbooks to understand and complete. Please talk to a member of the team if you are not sure about what this means for you.
Please note it is only intended as an introduction to healthy coping techniques used in Dialectical Behavioural Therapy from a psychoeducational perspective.
