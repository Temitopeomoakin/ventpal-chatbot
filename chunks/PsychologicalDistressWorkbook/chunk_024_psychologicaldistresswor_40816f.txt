PsychologicalDistressWorkbook
Try using the opposite action skill with an emotion you are experiencing when it is not helping you.
1. What is the emotion?
2. Is it justified?
3. What action urge do you feel? What is the emotion asking you to do?
4. Ask yourself whether the urge is effective.
Is it in line with your morals and goals?
5. Identify the opposite action if you want to change the emotion.
6. Act opposite all the way to the urge
7. Repeat acting opposite until the original emotion changes
