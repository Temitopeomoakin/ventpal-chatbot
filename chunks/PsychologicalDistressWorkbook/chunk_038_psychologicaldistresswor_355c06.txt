FAST—self respect effectiveness or sticking to your own values and beliefs while also getting what you want.
Fair Be fair to others and to yourself.
Apologies Don’t apologise unless it is warranted. It is not warranted for having an opinion, disagreeing or making a request.
Stick to values Don’t compromise your own values just to be liked or get what you want.
Truthful Don’t exaggerate or be dishonest about the situation.
