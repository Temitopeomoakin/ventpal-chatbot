It’s often easiest to get people to complete questionnaires while they’re taking part in an activity, but this could be disruptive or time-consuming. If you’re holding an individual or group discussion, how will you ask people to take part? • How much measurement you need to do. How many different outcomes can you realistically measure? Do you need to include everybody involved in an activity? If you’re working with a small number of people, you should try to involve them all. If you’re working with a large number, you may want to select a sample. 68
