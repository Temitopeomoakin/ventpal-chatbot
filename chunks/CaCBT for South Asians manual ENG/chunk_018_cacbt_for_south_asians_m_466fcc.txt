1.2 Religion and Spirituality
It is worth exploring the common beliefs held by South Asian individuals regarding the causes of mental illness as these often firmly held beliefs can potentially become barriers to therapy. In many cases, consideration of these factors in formulation and psychoeducation will result in better engagement. See Table 3 for details about widely held beliefs from South Asians about the causes of mental illness.
