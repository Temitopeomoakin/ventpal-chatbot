Physical Symptoms
South Asian clients often present with somatic complaints. In a typical psychiatry outpatient department, nearly 60% of depressed clients present with bodily complaints [70]. We often use the example of the increase in heartbeat or abdominal cramps in response to stressful situations to highlight the link between stress and bodily symptoms.
