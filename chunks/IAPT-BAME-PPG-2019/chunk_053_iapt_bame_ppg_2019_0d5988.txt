Staff training needs
Managers should invest in training for staff to support work with diverse communities. This might include training on work with specific groups or attending conferences where diversity issues were considered as a part of keynotes or symposia. This will encourage specific staff to develop particular areas of expertise. Any additional training should be supported through further consideration of these issues in supervision to embed this training in practice. Supervisors are also in a unique position to be able to comment on the training needs of therapists,
