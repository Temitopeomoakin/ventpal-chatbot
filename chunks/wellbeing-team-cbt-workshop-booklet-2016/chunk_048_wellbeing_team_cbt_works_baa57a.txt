Without distraction
When doing exposure it is important that people can experience some anxiety to see that it will reduce naturally. Therefore, it is important that people do not do other things,
or distract themselves with safety behaviors. This is also so your body learns it can cope without them. CBT Skills Training Book 39 Wellbeing Services
