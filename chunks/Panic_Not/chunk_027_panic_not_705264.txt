REVISE YOUR TERRIBLE THOUGHT
Considering the evidence for and against your terrible thought, it’s time to balance the evidence collected to create a Revised, More Balanced Thought.
n Ensure it’s a statement that considers all the evidence gathered.
n Write this thought down.
n As a percentage indicate how much you believe this new Revised, More Balanced thought.
