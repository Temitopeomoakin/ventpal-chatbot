Step 3. Make changes
Once you have identified the factors that may be contributing to your sleep difficulties, you can see where you will need to make changes.
This may include planning the time you will go to bed each night and the time you will get up each morning.
Step 4. Review the changes you made
Have you made the changes that you planned?
Did it help? If not, what could you do differently?
