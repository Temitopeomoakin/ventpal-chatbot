1. Square breathing
Introduction
To start off, this simple exercise can be useful in helping us pay attention to our breathing.
Before trying out the different relaxation techniques, you may wish to use this technique to help ensure that your breathing is even, smooth and at a comfortable pace for your body.
Close your eyes are try the exercise below:
1. Take a deep
2. Hold for four breath in for seconds four seconds
3. Breathe out
4. Hold for four for four seconds seconds
