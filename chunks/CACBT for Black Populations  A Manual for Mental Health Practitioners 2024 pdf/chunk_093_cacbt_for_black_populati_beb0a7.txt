Negative self‐talk contributes to negative emotions by colouring the way people perceive external events. The therapist can show the client how these negative thoughts are connected to negative feelings (e.g., being afraid, unsure or stressed) and negative behaviours (e.g., getting angry, giving up or blaming others). This can open their eyes to how changing that self‐talk can similarly promote changes to more positive feelings and behaviours. 56
