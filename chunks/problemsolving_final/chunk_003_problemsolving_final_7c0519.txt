Problem solving
The six steps
The next few pages of this workbook will guide you through the six steps of problem solving.
Step 1 – Write down the problem
Step 2 – Write down as many solutions as you can think of
Step 3 – Decide which solution is most effective
Step 4 – Choose a solution
Step 5 – Create an action plan
Step 6 – Evaluate
